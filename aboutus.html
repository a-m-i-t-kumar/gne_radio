About Us

Our History

Inauguration of FM Community Radio Station at GNDEC. Ludhiana, April 2, 2016: Prof (Dr.) Jaspal Singh Sandhu, Secretary, University Grants Commission, New Delhi launched the new FM Community Radio Station FM 90.8 MHz at Guru Nanak Dev Engineering College (GNDEC), Ludhiana.

The 90.8 FM Community Radio Station of Guru Nanak Dev Engineering College Ludhiana is an effort to serve the society by broadcasting programmes pertinent to education, information, entertainment, recreation, public affairs, culture, career guidance sports, etc.
